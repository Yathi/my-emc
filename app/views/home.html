ABOUT US

Edmonton Movie Club (EMC) is a non-profit film society registered under the Alberta Societies Act. EMC was formed in December 2012 to promote independent and regional cinema from India among the citizens of Alberta, with a special focus in Edmonton. The co-benefits of EMC include the following:

  • Provide wholesome entertainment for diverse population from India in the economically prosperous Province of Alberta and in particular, City of Edmonton. Entertainment brings joy and which in turn improves the quality of life;
  • Educate the local community about the diversity that exists among the Indian population. Understanding the diversity of the India community will in turn will bring cultural harmony and enable the local community to understand the culturally diverse Indian community;
  • Create awareness about key issues in India through documentary screening; and
  • Provide non-Bollywood Indian movies to all the people in Edmonton and Calgary.
